HOW TO CHANNEL A DOCTOR

  1. Select Your Specialist: Browse through our list of specialists and choose the doctor based on your medical needs.
  2. Choose a Convenient Time: View the available slots and select a date and time that fits your schedule.
  3. Fill in Your Details: Complete the required patient information form to confirm your appointment.
  4. Receive Confirmation: Once your appointment is booked, you’ll receive an instant confirmation via email or SMS with the details.
  5. Attend Your Appointment: Arrive at the hospital at your scheduled time, and our team will assist you with the check-in process.
