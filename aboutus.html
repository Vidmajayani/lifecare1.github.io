ABOUT US

Welcome to LifeCare Medical Hospital, your trusted partner in health and wellness. Established with a commitment to providing exceptional healthcare services, our hospital has grown to include four state-of-the-art branches across the Sri Lanka. Each of our branches is equipped with modern medical technology and staffed by highly skilled professionals dedicated to delivering personalized care.

At LifeCare Medical Hospital, we believe in a holistic approach to healthcare, where patient well-being is at the core of everything we do. Our wide range of services includes emergency care, specialized consultations, advanced surgical procedures, and comprehensive outpatient care, ensuring that we meet the diverse needs of our patients. We are committed to advancing medical knowledge and practices, fostering innovation in healthcare, and continuously improving the quality of our services to enhance patient outcomes.

BRANCHES

LIFECARE COLOMBO BRANCH

The Colombo Branch of our hospital stands as the flagship facility, situated in the bustling heart of Sri Lanka's capital city. As the largest branch, it offers an extensive array of specialized medical services, equipped with the latest advancements in medical technology. This branch is renowned for its comprehensive diagnostic and therapeutic services, addressing a wide spectrum of health conditions. It is strategically designed to accommodate the growing demands of a diverse patient base, ensuring timely and effective care. The Colombo Branch prides itself on its commitment to excellence and innovation in healthcare.

With a team of highly skilled medical professionals and support staff, the Colombo Branch is dedicated to providing exceptional care in a compassionate environment. The branch also focuses on continuous improvement and innovation to enhance patient outcomes and ensure the highest standards of medical practice.

  • 600+ beds available with 24/7 emergency care
  • Specialized units for cardiology, oncology, and neurology
  • Advanced diagnostic imaging and laboratory services
  • Specialized outpatient clinics and emergency care
  • State-of-the-art surgical facilities and recovery rooms
LIFECARE KANDY BRANCH

Nestled in the scenic city of Kandy, our Kandy Branch is a cornerstone of healthcare in the Central Province. It is strategically positioned to provide a range of medical services tailored to the local community and surrounding areas. This branch combines modern medical technology with a serene environment, creating a balanced approach to patient care. It is distinguished by its focus on personalized treatment plans and specialized care in various fields, making it a trusted choice for residents seeking reliable and compassionate healthcare services. The Kandy Branch is dedicated to maintaining high standards and a patient-centered approach.

Our dedicated team at the Kandy Branch ensures that patients receive the highest level of medical attention in a supportive and caring atmosphere. The branch is committed to offering top-notch healthcare services while maintaining a focus on patient satisfaction and well-being.

  • 500+ beds with private and semi-private rooms
  • Advanced surgical suites
  • Specialized clinics
  • Comprehensive diagnostic
  • Comfortable inpatient accommodations
LIFECARE GALLE BRANCH

Located in the historic city of Galle, our Galle Branch serves as a vital healthcare provider for the Southern Province. This branch integrates cutting-edge medical technology with a commitment to high-quality patient care. The Galle Branch is known for its specialized services and expert medical staff, offering a range of treatments designed to meet the diverse needs of the community. The facility's modern infrastructure and emphasis on patient comfort make it a preferred choice for those seeking comprehensive and compassionate medical services in a picturesque setting.

The Galle Branch emphasizes a patient-centered approach and strives to create a welcoming environment for all visitors. Our team is dedicated to providing compassionate care and utilizing the latest medical technologies to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

  • 450+ beds with dedicated outpatient facilities
  • Specialized care in orthopedics and trauma management
  • Full-service rehabilitation center and physiotherapy units
  • Outpatient clinics for various specialties
  • Advanced diagnostic tools and treatment options
  • Well-equipped emergency and critical care units
LIFECARE WATTALA BRANCH

The Wattala Branch, situated in the Northern suburbs of Colombo, offers a pivotal healthcare option for residents in the area. This branch provides an extensive range of medical services, equipped with contemporary facilities and advanced technology. The Wattala Branch is designed to deliver high-quality care with a focus on accessibility and patient satisfaction. It features a range of specialized departments and services aimed at addressing various health concerns. The branch is committed to offering a supportive and efficient healthcare experience, ensuring that patients receive the best possible care close to home.

With a focus on patient comfort and clinical excellence, the Wattala Branch is committed to delivering exceptional healthcare services. Our team of professionals works tirelessly to meet the diverse needs of our patients and provide a supportive and efficient healthcare experience.

  • 400+ beds with a focus on geriatric and chronic care
  • Comprehensive dialysis and nephrology services
  • Specialized medical departments and clinics
  • Full diagnostic and laboratory services
  • Comfortable and modern inpatient facilities
